## FAQ

**Does this affect open source contributors?**  
No. Contributors keep their MIT rights. This only applies to proprietary commercial use.

**Can I evaluate OpenOSINT before purchasing?**  
Yes. The MIT License covers evaluation, development, and testing in any context.

**What counts as commercial use requiring this license?**  
Shipping OpenOSINT as part of a closed-source product or SaaS to end users. Internal tooling is fine under MIT.
